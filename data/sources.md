# Data Sources

Public sources used for market sizing and the financial model.

## Sales data
- **SIAM (Society of Indian Automobile Manufacturers)** — annual reports FY22, FY23, FY24. https://www.siam.in/
- **Vahan Dashboard** — state and district-wise vehicle registration. https://vahan.parivahan.gov.in/vahan4dashboard/

## Company filings
- **Ola Electric DRHP** (August 2024) — cost structure, revenue breakdown, dealer network economics
- **Ather Energy Annual Report FY24** — cross-check on market share and revenue per unit
- **TVS Motor Annual Report FY24** — iQube unit economics

## Industry reports
- **NITI Aayog**, "India's Electric Mobility Transformation" (2023)
- **CRISIL**, "Battery Swapping in India: Powering the EV Revolution" (2024)
- **JMK Research**, monthly EV registration reports

## Policy / subsidy
- **FAME-II scheme guidelines** — Ministry of Heavy Industries
- **PLI Auto / Auto Components scheme** — Ministry of Heavy Industries
- **State EV policies** — Delhi, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Telangana (referenced for state-level subsidy stacking)

## Battery-swap infrastructure
- **Sun Mobility** public deck (2024) — station count, partnership model
- **Bounce Infinity** — public statements on swap network footprint
- **Battery Smart** — funding-round disclosures

## Notes
- BOM split and dealer-margin numbers are cross-checked across at least two sources where possible.
- FY27 projections in the notebook are my own, using 3% combined ICE+EV growth and an 18% EV-share assumption based on industry consensus.
- Some figures (especially battery-swap NPV) involve assumptions on subscriber churn and battery life — these are stated explicitly in the notebook.
